How scores work
Unlike stars on Google or Yelp, Nayna scores
are earned — every point is backed by a real visit.
📅
Client books via Nayna
Booking creates a receipt tied to the client's verified identity. No receipt = no review.
✦
Review unlocks post-visit
48 hours after the appointment, Nayna prompts the client to score across 4 dimensions — skill, communication, timing, value.
◈
Score updates live
The score is a rolling weighted average — recent reviews carry more weight. Artists can't pay to change it.
